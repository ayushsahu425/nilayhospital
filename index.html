Our Services

At Nilay Hospital, we provide a comprehensive range of inpatient (IPD) and outpatient (OPD) medical services, designed to deliver high-quality and compassionate care across all major specialties. Our IPD services include emergency casualty care, general medicine, neonatology and pediatrics, neuro medicine, obstetrics and gynecology, orthopedics, general and gastro surgery, urology, oncology, dialysis, and pain medicine. For our OPD patients, we offer specialized consultations in all of the above disciplines along with dedicated neurosurgery and nephrology services.
To enhance patient comfort and convenience, we also offer essential facilities such as cardiac ambulance services, clean drinking water, a hygienic canteen, parking space, and accessible toilets. Please note that radiation therapy, transplant services, and cath-lab or cardiac surgeries are currently not available at our facility. We are committed to continuously upgrading our infrastructure and service offerings to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our Mission

At Nilay Hospital, our mission is to restore health and renew hope by delivering comprehensive healthcare with a human touch. We are committed to:

  • Establishing advanced infrastructure to support complete and compassionate patient care.
  • Maintaining a high standard of medical expertise through a dedicated team of experienced doctors and faculty.
  • Evolving into a centre of excellence with state-of-the-art facilities, offering multiple super specialties under one roof. Our environment supports medical training, academic research, and continuous professional development.

Our Vision

We envision a future where quality healthcare is accessible, ethical, and compassionate. Our goal is:

  • To offer reliable, affordable, and approachable medical services for all.
  • To serve with sincerity, respect, and sensitivity towards every patient and family.
  • To promote public health through awareness, education, and prevention of diseases and injuries, ultimately contributing to a healthier society.

Our Core Values

At the heart of Nilay Hospital are values that guide every action and decision:

  • Excellence – Striving for the highest quality in patient care and clinical outcomes.
  • Integrity – Acting with honesty, transparency, and strong moral principles.
  • Compassion – Providing care with empathy, kindness, and respect.
  • Accountability – Taking responsibility for our services and commitments.
  • Ethics – Upholding ethical standards in every aspect of our practice.
