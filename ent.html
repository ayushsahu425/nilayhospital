ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat) Care Department
Dr.

At Nilay Hospital, our ENT Department provides comprehensive care for all ear, nose, and throat conditions in both children and adults. From common problems like ear infections and sinusitis to advanced treatments for hearing loss and head & neck surgeries, we are committed to restoring comfort, clarity, and quality of life for our patients.

Our team of ENT specialists uses advanced diagnostic tools and modern treatment methods to ensure accurate evaluation and effective care. Whether it is a routine consultation, a minor procedure, or a complex surgery, patients receive compassionate and specialized attention in a safe and supportive environment.

Our ENT Services

Ear Care

  • Treatment of infections, pain, and hearing loss
  • Management of tinnitus and balance disorders
  • Hearing tests and hearing aid fittings
  • Micro ear surgeries (Tympanoplasty, Mastoidectomy)

Nose & Sinus Care

  • Treatment for sinusitis, allergies, and polyps
  • Endoscopic sinus surgeries (FESS)
  • Correction of deviated septum (Septoplasty)
  • Management of nasal fractures and nosebleeds

Throat & Voice Care

  • Treatment of tonsillitis and adenoid problems
  • Management of voice disorders and hoarseness
  • Microlaryngeal surgeries for vocal cords
  • Treatment of sleep apnea and snoring

Head & Neck Services

  • Evaluation of swellings and thyroid disorders
  • Salivary gland disease management
  • Surgical management of head & neck tumors
  • Comprehensive cancer screenings

At Nilay Hospital, we believe in providing patient-centered ENT care that not only treats the condition but also enhances the overall quality of life. From improving hearing and breathing to restoring voice and comfort, our ENT department ensures specialized care tailored to each patient’s needs.
